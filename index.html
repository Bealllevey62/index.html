Xatanical Downloader is the most controversial project in today science. The goal of Xatanical is to provide free and unrestricted access to all scientific knowledge ever published in journal or book form.

Today the circulation of knowledge in science is restricted by high prices. Many students and researchers cannot afford academic journals and books that are locked behind paywalls.

Xatanical is helping millions of students and researchers, medical professionals, journalists and curious people in all countries to unlock access to knowledge. The mission donwloader is to fight every obstacle that prevents open access to knowledge: be it legal, technical or otherwise.
